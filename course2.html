Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Description: Welcome to the world of cybersecurity! In this introductory online course, you will embark on a journey to gain insights into the fundamental concepts, terminologies, and principles of cybersecurity. Understand the critical importance of information security, recognize common cyber threats, and learn essential security measures that are vital in our digital age. With a duration of 6 hours, this course will equip you with the foundational knowledge needed to navigate the cybersecurity landscape.

Course Outline

  • Module 1: Introduction to Cybersecurity
  • Module 2: Threat Landscape: Malware, Phishing, and Social Engineering
  • Module 3: Network Security Basics
  • Module 4: Password Management and Authentication
  • Module 5: Security Awareness and Best Practices
